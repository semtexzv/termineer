Termineer

Your self-directing coding engineer in the terminal. Termineer is like having a dedicated developer on your team that can design, implement, and debug code autonomously. As a Project Manager, think of Termineer as your engineer who takes initiative and delivers complete solutions, not just suggestions.

Built in just one week using itself, Termineer can analyze codebases, create new features, refactor existing code, write tests, and integrate APIs – all through natural conversation with vibe coding.
